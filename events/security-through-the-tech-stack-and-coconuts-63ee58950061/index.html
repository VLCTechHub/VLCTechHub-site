Valencia Codes would like to welcome you all to our #10 edition of Level Up at the comfy offices of commercetools.

What is Level Up? It's a monthly meet-up where we will be inviting speakers from inside and outside of our community to introduce you to interesting technical topics and share their experiences with you. Each talk will be between 20-25 minutes + 5 more minutes for Q&A.

Of Coconuts & Security by Denis Sellu, Engineering Lead - Website @ Ably Realtime Just like how the layers of a Coconut protect the precious seed, the layers of our stack also protect something…. Let’s talk about why we should care about security, what we are protecting, and actions we can take to build secure applications, from the Physical device we build them on, to cloud services we run them on, there are basic steps we can take to make sure our applications have the most basic protection. Also, understand the repercussions of what can happen if these actions are not taken….and learn about Coconuts
