Galeo Tech in collaboration with Barbara IoT and Confluent present this new meetup in the field of Industry 4.0.

In this new virtual event, we will discuss knowledge, experiences and challenges related to the use of IoT technology in the industry.

We will discuss the life cycle of in-plant devices, industrial data governance and streaming analytics.

Join digital industry technologists, developers, and experts in a discussion-oriented forum to learn from a practical perspective how to extract value from data in industry 4.0 initiatives.

We will wait for you!

Agenda:

  • Welcome introduction Marco Laucelli. Partner and co-founder of Galeo Tech

  • Industrial IoT easy and secure Juan Pérez_Bedmar Férnandez. VP Sales and Marketing, Barbara IoT

  • The Complete Event Streaming Platform for Apache Kafka Sergio Durán Vegas. Systems Engineer, Iberia at Confluent

  • Workshop: Integrated Data Governance in Industrial IoT Alejandro Alija, Partner, and co-founder of Galeo Tech.

Due to the current health crisis of Covid-19, the meeting will be held online with no initial capacity limit.
