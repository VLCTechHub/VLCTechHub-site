Join us at the QA Minds Europe launch event! September 28th will be a day packed with interesting talks from different well known speakers in the testing community.

The QA Minds Europe launch event will start at 10AM , with a keynote from:

Simon Stewart (Lead Committer, Selenium Project & Creator of WebDriver).

The event will close around 5PM with another keynote from :

Dan Cuellar (Creator of Appium).

During the day there will be different talks from testing and automation experts, such as:

Andrew Krug (founder of Lazy Coder and Selenium expert)

Jose Pita (experienced mobile automation engineer @ Wikimedia)

Noemí Ferrera ( Senior Software Engineer)

Diego Molina (creator of Zalenium, Selenium Committer, Software Engineer @ Sauce Labs)

Blanca Moreno (co-founder QA Minds Latin America & Europe)

Jesús Sánchez (QA Lead @ Onestic)

After the launch event’s end, there will be a space for beers, food and networking.

This event is cost free, then only step needed to assist is: register and join us!

What is QA Minds Europe?

QA Minds Europe is the extension of QA Minds Latin America, which is a company and a community with a wide and recognized testing background in Latin America. We are a network of specialists and professionals who spread their knowledge and share experiences related to software testing and quality assurance through different talks and events across Europe.

In addition to that, we help to keep different companies and individuals stay up to date in testing tools and techniques through high value trainings, and with this, we transform our students in excellent software testers. Our instructors and mentors are specialists in software testing who are daily practitioners in their jobs at some of the best digital companies in the world.

Why QA Minds Europe?

Quality is a task that remains pending in most companies, and in many cases there is not a clear philosophy regarding this matter. For this reason, we have decided to expand from Latin America to Europe, keeping the same collaborative spirit, sharing and offering knowledge and skills on this field.

Our goal is to offer different types of testing via several events and workshops throughout the year. We will focus our initial efforts on our launch event and three different workshops that we have built based on references and feedback from the testing community, always concentrated on delivering high value content to fulfill our attendant’s expectations.

Workshops

Initially, we will offer the following workshops:

September 30th: 09:00am - 17:00pm

October 1st: 09:00am - 17:00pm

Automation with Selenium - Andrew Krug (founder of Lazy Coder and Selenium expert)

Testing Mobile Apps with Appium - Jose Pita (experienced mobile automation engineer @ Wikimedia)

Simplifying and improving automated testing with Docker - Diego Molina (Selenium Committer, Software Engineer @ Sauce Labs)
