BigML’s Summer 2017 release brings Deepnets, a highly effective supervised learning method that solves classification and regression problems in a way that can match or exceed human performance, especially in domains where effective feature engineering is difficult. Deepnets are state-of-the-art in speech recognition, text classification, image classification, and object detection tasks, among other use cases.

Please join us for a FREE webinar to find out how Deepnets work. The live webinar will be on Thursday October 5, 2017, at 07:00 PM CEST (Valencia, Spain. GMT +02:00) and it will be held in English.
