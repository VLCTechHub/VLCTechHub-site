The BigML Team is about to launch the first release of the year on Wednesday, January 31, 2018, at 07:00 PM CET via an ONLINE WEBINAR. The attendance is free, but you need to reserve your spot to gain access.

The new release presents operating thresholds, a feature that allows you to tune the performance of your classification model to your specific use case. BigML operating thresholds, available from the BigML Dashboard, API, and WhizzML, give you the power to be more or less aggressive when applying certain classes to an instance, which is especially useful in domains like fraud detection or medical diagnosis where the consequences of some classifications may be very different. Additionally, BigML will showcase organizations, a convenient collaborative space where several customers can work on the same projects, within the same Dashboard, at different levels of privileges. Organizations break down barriers and make it easy and efficient for any company to adopt Machine Learning across their entire corporate structure.

The LIVE webinar will be held in English.
